About the Forming and Shaping Technology (FAST) Diffraction Planning Tool

The web-based FAST Diffraction Planning Tool is an extension of a Python-based application, designed to assist users at Cornell High Energy Synchrotron Source (CHESS). It enables visualization and decision-making for optimizing experimental setups.

To enhance the analysis of material microstructures and gain valuable insights into their mechanical properties, the tool is designed to assist researchers and scientists.
